Prime Minister's Special Scholarship Scheme

Empowering education through a seamless, paperless scholarship system.

Get Started

Digital Submission

Submit all required documents through our secure online portal.

Automated Verification

Your documents are automatically forwarded to the SAG Bureau for verification.

Paperless Disbursement

The entire process is paperless, with scholarships disbursed directly to your account.

How It Works

Step 1: Register and Upload Documents

Students register on the portal and upload required documents for the scholarship process.

Step 2: Verification by SAG Bureau

Documents are reviewed and verified by the SAG Bureau to ensure compliance.

Step 3: Forwarding to Finance Bureau

Once verified, the SAG Bureau forwards the documents to the Finance Bureau for payment processing.

Step 4: Scholarship Disbursement

Upon approval, scholarships are directly deposited into students' bank accounts.
